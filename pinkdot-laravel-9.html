Description
Booking System for Lab Tests with Medical Approval
Doctor's Approval for Lab Test Bookings:

Patients can book lab tests through the system.

These bookings require approval from doctors or medical professionals.

Accessibility for Both Genders under Medical Guidance:

The platform ensures accessibility for both female and male patients.

Medical guidance helps tailor the accessibility according to specific needs.

Counselor Validation of Patient Documents:

Patients must submit necessary documents for validation.

Counselors verify and validate these documents for accuracy.

Rejection of Bookings with Invalid Documents:

The system automatically rejects bookings with invalid or incorrect documents.

This ensures data accuracy and prevents erroneous bookings.

Notification for Document Corrections:

Patients receive notifications for necessary corrections in their submitted documents.

This facilitates timely rectification and ensures smooth processing.

Booking Confirmation upon Document Validation:

Once documents are validated, bookings are confirmed for the scheduled lab tests.

Administrative Management Features:

Counselors Management: Administrative control over counselors' accounts and activities.

Laboratories Management: Admin oversight and management of registered laboratories.

Permitted ID Proofs: Establishment and maintenance of a database for permitted ID proofs.

Comprehensive Patient Activity Logs Monitoring:

Admins have access to detailed patient activity logs.

This allows comprehensive monitoring and tracking of patient interactions and system usage.

System Benefits:

Efficiency: Streamlines the process of booking lab tests while ensuring accuracy and validity through document validation.

Accessibility: Accommodates both male and female patients while adhering to medical guidance.

Accuracy: Automatically rejects bookings with incorrect or invalid documents, ensuring data accuracy.

Transparency: Patients receive notifications for necessary document corrections, promoting transparency in the process.

Administrative Control: Provides administrative oversight and management of counselors, laboratories, and ID proofs.

Monitoring and Security: Detailed patient activity logs enable monitoring and enhance system security.

This system facilitates a seamless, secure, and efficient process for patients to book lab tests under medical guidance while ensuring data accuracy and compliance.
